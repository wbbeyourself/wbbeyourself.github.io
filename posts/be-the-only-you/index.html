发掘独到知识，成为无可替代的自己

Many of you might be worried about being replaced by machines

你们中的许多人可能会担心被机器

or by countless other talented individuals.

或其他无数有才能的人取代。

But have you ever paused to think

但是，你有没有静下心来想一想，

that when you develop your specific knowledge,

当你掌握了独到的专业知识，

there’s no one out there who can truly compete with you?

就没有人能够真正与你竞争了。

This isn’t the kind of knowledge you get in school

这不是你在学校

or during a training program

或培训中获得的知识。

You know, if society can train you,

要知道，如果社会可以培训你，

it can train someone else too.

它也可以培训别人。

Where can you get specific knowledge?

你从哪里可以获得独到知识？

It’s already within you.

它就在你身上。

You just need to recognize it and home it.

你只需要认出它，磨炼它。

This knowledge stems from your very genes,

这些知识源于你的基因、

your upbringing, the essence of who you are.

你的成长经历和你本来的样子。

It’s part of your personality and your identity.

它是你性格和身体的一部分。

It lies in the things that you kept doing

它存在于你小时候

as a kid or teenager effortlessly.

或青少年时期毫不费力就能做到的事情中。

It might be something you didn’t consider a skill,

也许你并不认为这是一项技能，

but you did it way better than people around you.

但你做得比周围的人都要好。

Let’s say your family has a rich sports history.

比方说，你的家族有着丰富的体育历史。

It’s no surprise then if you have a natural affinity for sports.

那么你对体育运动有一种天生的亲和力，就不足为奇了。

These talents often run in the blood.

这种天赋往往流淌在你的血液中。

Or maybe you’re that person who can walk into a room

又或者，你是那种走进一个房间，

and, with just a few words,

三言两语

have everyone nodding in agreement.

就能让大家点头称是的人。

While an introvert might struggle in sales,

性格内向的人(i人)可能会在销售领域举步维艰，

you, with your extroverted nature, could thrive.

而性格外向的你(e人)却能茁壮成长。

Now, some of you might be thinking,

现在，有些人可能会想，

“I haven’t found my unique talent or skill.”

“我还没有找到自己独特的天赋或技能。”

It’s a feeling many of us can relate to.

我们很多人都会有这种感觉。

More often than not,

更多时候，

societal expectations or our own self-doubts

社会的期望或我们的自我怀疑

prevent us from seeing our stengths.

阻碍了我们看到自己的优势。

But dig deep, get to know yourself better,

但是，深入挖掘，更好地了解自己，

and you’re bound to find it.

你就能找到它。

And once you find it,

一旦你找到了它，

align that knowledge with

就把这些知识与

what society needs but does not know how to get it.

社会需要但不知道如何获取的点结合起来。

That’s when you’ll create immense value

这时，你就会创造出巨大的价值，

and truly shine.

真正大放异彩。

Remember, no one can ever compete with you at being you.

请记住，在做你自己这一点上，没有人能与你竞争。
